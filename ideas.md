# Snoochiez — Design Brainstorm

## Brand Essence
Cannabis cultivation & lifestyle brand. Loud. Authentic. High-end but unfiltered. Anti-corporate. Culture-first.

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalism meets Street Art Zine Culture

**Core Principles:**
- Raw, unapologetic energy — thick borders, stark contrast, intentional "imperfection"
- High-end quality signal through restraint: one bold typeface, one accent color
- Asymmetric layouts that feel hand-assembled, like a zine or skate mag
- Texture-forward: grain overlays, paper-like surfaces, ink-stamp aesthetics

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black charcoal (#0E0D0B) — premium, moody, late-night
- Primary accent: electric lime-green (#C8FF00) — loud, alive, cannabis culture
- Secondary: warm off-white (#F5F0E8) — aged paper, authenticity
- Emotional intent: tension between darkness and electric energy

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Off-grid, staggered card placements with heavy typographic blocks
- Hero: full-bleed dark background, oversized display text offset to the left
- Products: masonry-style grid with thick lime borders on hover
- No centered hero text — always offset, always dynamic

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick 2-3px lime-green borders used as dividers and hover states
- Ink-stamp / distressed texture overlays on section backgrounds
- Bold all-caps section headers with letter-spacing

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: lime border flash + slight scale
- Page transitions: fast horizontal slide
- Cursor: custom crosshair in lime green

**Animation:**
- Entrance: staggered text reveal from bottom (transform + opacity)
- Scroll: parallax on hero image
- Cards: border-color transition on hover (0.15s ease)

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Bebas Neue" — ultra-condensed, loud, all-caps for headers
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean, modern, readable
- Accent: "Space Mono" — monospaced for labels, prices, tags

## Selected Design: Neo-Brutalism meets Street Art Zine Culture

**Rationale:** Best captures Snoochiez's dual identity — high-end cultivation quality + raw, unfiltered personality. The electric lime on near-black creates immediate visual impact and distinctiveness. Bebas Neue + DM Sans gives authority without pretension.
